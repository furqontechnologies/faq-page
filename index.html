Al-Furqon Tahfeedh School FAQ

What is the admission process?

The admission process involves filling out an application form, attending an interview, and completing any necessary assessments.

What are the school hours?

School hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

Are uniforms required?

Yes, students are required to wear the school uniform.

What is Qur'an Tahfeedh and why is it important?

Qur'an Tahfeedh is the memorization of the Qur'an, and it's important for preserving the words of Allah, spiritual growth, and closeness to Him.

What teaching methods are employed in our Qur'an Tahfeedh school?

We utilize traditional methods such as repetition, recitation, and personalized instruction with qualified teachers.

What age groups do we cater to in our Qur'an Tahfeedh program?

Our program accommodates students of various age groups, typically starting from age 5 or 6.

How long does it generally take for a student to memorize the entire Qur'an?

The time varies for each student, but it usually takes several years of consistent effort and practice.

Are there any prerequisites for enrollment in our Qur'an Tahfeedh school?

Students should have a basic understanding of Arabic and be able to recite the Qur'an fluently with proper pronunciation.

Do we provide certification upon completion of the Qur'an Tahfeedh program?

Yes, we offer certification upon the completion of memorizing the entire Qur'an or at various milestones of memorization.

What kind of support do we offer to students who may struggle with memorization?

We provide individualized support, additional resources, and encouragement to students facing challenges in memorization.

Do we offer online Qur'an Tahfeedh classes for remote learners?

Yes, we provide online classes to accommodate students who are unable to attend in person.

How can parents support their children enrolled in our Qur'an Tahfeedh program?

Parents can create a conducive learning environment at home, encourage regular practice, and show enthusiasm for their child's progress.

Are there any extracurricular activities offered at our Qur'an Tahfeedh school?

Yes, we offer extracurricular activities such as Islamic lectures, community service projects, and Qur'an competitions.

How can parents enroll their children in our Qur'an Tahfeedh school?

Parents can enroll their children by contacting us directly or visiting our website to learn about the enrollment process and requirements.
